The platform

Everything your firm needs to onboard clients — without the patchwork.

Structured client discovery

Intake forms capture what you need before the first conversation. Clients fill in details on their own time — you start every meeting with context, not a blank page.

AI meeting notes — any format

Record in-person, phone or video meetings. ClientFlow transcribes, identifies key details and populates the fact-find automatically. No re-typing from handwritten notes or separate apps.

Intelligent document generation

Suitability reports, risk profiles and fee disclosures drafted from connected client data — structured to your firm's practices. You review, edit and approve every document before it reaches the client.

Automated follow-ups

Reminders, outstanding items and next steps are tracked and sent without manual chasing. Clients know exactly what's needed. Your team stays focused on advice, not admin.

Client portal — one place for everything

A branded space where clients complete KYC/AML verification (Thirdfort, Credas), review documents and sign — with full data protection built in. No scattered emails, no confusion about what comes next.

Synced with your practice tools

Intelliflo, Wealthbox, Salesforce and similar practice management systems stay current. Bidirectional data sync means no double entry and no records falling out of date.

How it works

Five steps. One connected flow.

Each step feeds the next. No re-typing, no switching systems, no chasing.

1

Client completes intake

Your client receives a branded intake form and fills it in before the meeting. Structured data is ready when you sit down — no blank pages, no back-and-forth.

2

Meeting captured, fact-find populated

Record the meeting — in person, phone or video. ClientFlow transcribes, extracts key details and populates the fact-find. You review and enrich before anything else moves.

3

Documents generated, you approve

Suitability reports and compliance documents are drafted from connected client data, structured to your firm's practices. You edit and approve every document in Copilot before it goes anywhere.

4

Client completes KYC and signs

Your client verifies identity through Thirdfort or Credas, reviews documents and signs — all in one branded portal. Consumer Duty evidence and audit trail are logged automatically.

5

Practice systems updated

Intelliflo, Wealthbox, Salesforce or your practice management system synced automatically. Annual review timer set. Onboarding complete — nothing left to chase.
