What ClientFlow does

Six capabilities. One connected system.

Not a report writer. Not a CRM plug-in. The operating layer that connects client discovery to signed, compliant onboarding — without the fragmentation.

Smart client intake

Branded intake forms capture everything you need from the first interaction. Structured, complete, and directly connected to every step that follows — no re-entry, no missing fields chased later.

AI meeting notes

Upload any recording — in-person audio, video call, or phone. ClientFlow transcribes and structures it into meeting notes and client information automatically. Your discovery conversation becomes organised client data without a single keystroke.

Document generation, your way

Suitability letters, fee disclosures and compliance documents are generated from real client data — structured around your firm's standards. You review, edit and approve every draft before anything reaches a client. The advice is always yours.

Automated follow-ups

Timely nudges keep clients moving and your team informed without manual chasing. Outstanding action prompts, annual review alerts and progress reminders fire automatically — nothing falls through between meetings.

Guided client portal

Your clients get one secure place — not a scatter of emails and links. Fact-find, KYC and identity verification (Thirdfort or Credas), document signing and data consent all happen inside, presented in a clear sequence. GDPR-compliant. Professionally presented.

FCA compliance & practice sync

Consumer Duty evidence and audit trail are captured on every interaction. Syncs with Intelliflo and similar practice management tools so your system of record stays current — no duplicate entry, no compliance gaps.

Why this matters

Great advisors are buried in admin. It shouldn't be this way.

We had a great discovery meeting. Then I spent three hours turning notes into a fact-find, writing a suitability report from scratch, and chasing the client for their ID. Same thing every time. Every client.

  • Every discovery call produces notes that sit in a separate app, disconnected from documentation. Converting them into a client record and a compliant report is entirely manual — every time.

  • Suitability reports start from a blank page or a generic prompt. Hours of writing, referencing and editing for every client and every annual review — even when the underlying data is the same.

  • KYC, Consumer Duty evidence and compliance records live across drives, inboxes and disconnected systems. When the regulator asks for evidence, the search begins across all of them.

The cost isn't just time — it's the client experience and the compliance risk that comes with doing it all manually.

How it works

From first meeting to fully onboarded — in one connected flow.

Every step feeds into the next. What the client completes in their portal becomes your data. What you record in a meeting becomes the foundation of the suitability report. Nothing is re-entered.

1
Client

Discovery & structured intake

The client receives a link to your branded portal and completes a structured fact-find at their own pace. No paper forms, no Word attachments. Their answers flow directly into your onboarding workflow — organised, complete and ready to use.

2
Advisor

Meeting notes, generated from any recording

Upload your discovery meeting recording — in-person audio, video call or phone. ClientFlow transcribes, identifies key client details and adds structured notes directly to the client profile. You review, enrich and confirm. Nothing to type from scratch.

3
Client

KYC, identity & data consent in the portal

Identity verification (Thirdfort or Credas) and data consent are completed inside the same client portal — in a clear step-by-step sequence. No separate links. No chasing. The results flow directly into the compliance record, ready for the regulator.

4
Advisor

Suitability documents drafted from real data

The suitability letter and compliance documentation are generated from the connected fact-find and your recommendation — structured around your firm's standards. Not a blank prompt. Not a template you fill in. A genuine first draft from the data you already have.

5
Advisor

You review, edit and approve

Every document passes through your review before anything is sent. Read it, adjust the language, override any section. ClientFlow drafts; you decide. Once approved, the report and fee disclosure go to the client. The advice is always yours.

6
Client

Signed, evidenced and synced

The client signs digitally. Consumer Duty evidence is logged automatically. Intelliflo or your practice management tool is updated. The annual review is scheduled. Onboarding is complete — fully auditable, nothing missing, nothing scattered.
