How it works

One connected chain from brief to delivered engagement.

Intake, proposals, documents, delivery and client portal — in one flow.

1

Client completes branded intake portal

Your client submits a discovery questionnaire before the first call. Structured data is ready when you sit down — no blank pages, no back-and-forth to fill in the gaps.

2

Discovery call transcribed, brief auto-populated

Record the call — in person, phone or video. Smart Onboard transcribes, extracts key details and auto-populates the discovery brief. You review and enrich before the proposal begins.

3

Proposal drafted, you approve

Your recommendation feeds the Proposal Agent. A full proposal is drafted in your firm's voice — fee structure, scope, timeline and deliverables. You edit and approve before anything is sent.

4

Client accepts — SOW generated and signed

Client accepts the proposal. SOW auto-generated from the agreed terms — you review and edit with AI if needed, then send for e-signature. No retyping, no blank template.

5

Client portal live, engagement tracking active

SOW signed. Client portal goes live. Engagement tracker active. Deliverables, milestones and communications in one place — from first brief to final handover.
